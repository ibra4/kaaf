The Messenger of God, may God’s prayers and peace be upon him, said, “I and the sponsor of an orphan will be in Paradise like these two, and he pointed to the index and middle fingers, and separated them.” narrated by Muslim

The number of orphans sponsored by the Charitable Activities Committee has reached more than 10,000 orphans inside and outside Bahrain (22 countries). These orphans reside with their families or in orphanages affiliated with reliable educational bodies.

The committee supervises orphans who are sponsored outside Bahrain in cooperation and coordination with some well-known charitable societies and institutions in the international arena, and it also conducts periodic inspection visits to these institutions with the aim of following up on orphans abroad.

This project was launched with the beginning of the committee’s work in 1970, and since 1991 the Charitable Activities Committee has systematically implemented the project of sponsoring orphans, which has achieved great benefits for thousands of orphans whose faces were filled with joy and prayed to their sponsors with goodness and blessing.

The committee has many examples of distinguished graduates at the high school or university level, and some of them became a donor .., and all of that with the blessing of the hand of charity that extended to them and nurtured them through this project.

Sponsoring orphans has many dimensions. In addition to the great reward of the Lord of the worlds, sponsoring orphans contributes to the reform of society when the orphan becomes a good individual and is protected from displacement and loss.

What happens after the sponsorship: When you become a sponsor of the orphan, you will receive a photo of him, and after that you will receive regular reports about him to see the progress of the orphan’s progress first-hand, and the committee will make every effort to meet his living needs and improve his educational, educational, social and health conditions.

How to sponsor an orphan: The Charitable Activities Committee of the Islah Association gives you a unique opportunity to save these orphans through the Orphan Sponsorship Project, and in order to sponsor one or more orphans, you can contact us in the Charitable Works Committee of the Islah Association at phone 17333090 or the hotline 36006166, where one of the delegates will Orphans sponsorship by providing you with all the required information about the orphans whom you want to sponsor. The value of the monthly sponsorship for an orphan, according to his country Countries in which the project will be implemented: Bahrain, Palestine, Sudan, Albania, Ukraine, Kosovo, Iraq, Mauritania, India, Yemen, Eritrea, Afghanistan, Pakistan, Somalia, Sri Lanka.
